Foreign-Born Population per State vs. Healthcare Providers

Analysis

Based on the graph, we can tell that the number of foreign-born Africans and the population without a healthcare provider is negatively correlated. The variables move in opposite directions. As one variable increases, the other variable decreases. States with a higher foreign-born African population also have less individuals without a healthcare provider. There may be other factors at play. Further areas of study could determine wether the foreign-born African populations across the states are included within the healthcare provider reports and what the healthcare policies are like across states.
